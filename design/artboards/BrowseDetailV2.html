SAND
What it's for

Washed sand graded to ASTM C33 for ready-mix concrete.

Concrete Sand is a clean, washed sand sieved and graded to the ASTM C33 specification for fine aggregate. Fines are dropped, oversized particles are screened out, and the gradation is held within published bands so cement and water hydrate the mix predictably across the truck.

Used wherever the structural strength of poured concrete matters: foundation footings, slabs on grade, columns, sidewalks, and ready-mix work where the batch plant requires C33-spec sand. Also goes into mortar mixes, masonry leveling beds, and as a structural leveling layer under flagstone or retaining-wall caps.
